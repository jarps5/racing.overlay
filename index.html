Free · Browser-only · No install

Racing Overlay

Map a track, record laps with your phone's GPS, and stream live speed, delta and sector times straight into OBS. Everything runs in the browser — nothing to install in the car.

Use the same room code on the phone (Recorder) and the PC (Overlay) so they talk to each other. Anything else on this page works without a room code.

01 — In the car

Track Editor

Drive a lap to auto-map the track, then place sector splits and start/finish.
02 — In the car

Recorder

Live speed, delta and sector timing on your phone. Streams to the overlay.
03 — OBS / PC

Overlay

Transparent live overlay — add as a Browser Source in OBS.
04 — After the session

Analytics

Compare laps, speed traces and sector times. Export/import as JSON.

First time? Open js/firebase-config.js in this project and paste in a free Firebase Realtime Database config — that's what carries the live data from the phone to OBS. Full steps are in README.md.
